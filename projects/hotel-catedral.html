Hotel Catedral

Commercial
Location
La Paz, México
Size
1,730.25 m2 lot4,809.07 m2 under roof
Year
2016
Located in the historic center of La Paz, Hotel Catedral honors the tones, textures, and scale of its location while providing a modern, elegant retreat for travelers.

Just as the hotel sits on a leafy central town square, the hotel’s rooms are arranged around an open-air central courtyard, allowing for external views in every room.

Originally conceived as a lush space lined with foliage and blooms to create a living center of the building, the courtyard offers casual seating for guests as well as an outdoor bar area and entrance to the restaurant.

A light blue, external glass elevator extends like a waterfall into the courtyard, echoing the shape and blue color of the long rooftop pool above.

The sunny roof deck overlooks the Cathedral at La Paz. The modern lines of the building in glass, concrete, and stone frame the views across the city.
