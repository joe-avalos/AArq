Villa Bellissima

Residential
Location
Cabo San Lucas, México
Size
2,582.26 m2 lot1,501.78 m2 under roof
Year
2008
Villa Bellissima crowns the peak of a mountain in Pedregal, Cabo San Lucas.

The structure was virtually constructed in the open air because of the sixty-plus degree natural slope of the land. It consists of five horizontal planes, bisected with a vertical volume.

With most exterior walls made of sliding glass, the space is flooded in natural light. Looking out from the living areas, it feels as if the home is floating in the sky.

The expansive primary level of this nine-bedroom, eleven bath home includes the living and entertaining spaces, a gourmet kitchen, broad terraces with two infinity pools, and a jacuzzi.

Built with large events in mind, there is a second commercial kitchen, as well as magnificent indoor and outdoor dining and entertaining areas.

An elevator connects the five levels. The nine ocean-front bedroom suites are distributed among three levels, so each is just above or below the main level of the residence. Each bedroom has a private patio or balcony.

There is a private movie theater, high-end gym, a large office space, and generous parking area for cars, jet skis, and motorcycles.

We create lifestyles.”
