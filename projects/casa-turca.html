Casa Turca

Residential
Location
Cabo San Lucas, México
Size
481.26 m2 lot507.86 m2 under roof
Year
2013
Located on a spectacular view lot in the private neighborhood of Pedregal, Cabo San Lucas, Casa Turca is an open, modern home built with functionality and family in mind.

With a low-profile street level facade, the home opens boldly along its hillside lot, taking full advantage of its ocean views.

The design captures the Mediterranean tastes of its owners, with contemporary Baja roots. It blends welcoming, open gathering spaces with calm private spaces.

A garden bed planted with palms and trees along one wall of the living room extends along the exterior wall through sliding doors, drawing the eye toward the pool and sea beyond it, and connecting the indoor and outdoor space into one seamless room.

Nature in shades of greens and blues is ever present in this home: every room looks into a courtyard, planted garden, terrace, or toward the sea.

Outdoors, the bold horizontal lines, thick walls, and shades of bright whites and deep blues frame the Baja coastline.

“We do not design spaces.
We create lifestyles.”
